Choose how you want to store your todos

Explore different browser storage strategies side‑by‑side, powered by Storion.

LS

LocalStorage

Fast and simple key‑value storage.

Todos are persisted with Storion using the browser’s localStorage API. Great for lightweight use‑cases.

  • Persistence Browser‑scoped, per origin
  • Best for Small datasets, quick demos
  • Route /local-storage
IDB

IndexedDB

Structured storage for larger data.

Todos are persisted with Storion using indexedDB, giving you a more scalable, queryable store.

  • Persistence Indexed database in the browser
  • Best for Larger datasets, richer querying
  • Route /indexed-db
